Alterations & Returns

Home/Alteration & Return Policy

1. Custom Sized Goods are Final

Due to the hand-woven fabrics, natural color vat dyes, and custom zardozi/chikankari embroidery hours allocated for individual fits, Rang Mahal does not offer refunds, exchanges, or returns of any custom-tailored lehengas, sherwanis, sarees, or suits once embroidery design is confirmed.

2. Complimentary Lifetime Alterations

We guarantee a lifetime alteration warranty. If your body dimensions shift over the years, you may bring your Rang Mahal attire to our flagship Jaipur showroom, or our Mumbai and Delhi styling suites. Our tailors will adjust borders, chest sizing, sleeves, and panels free of charge.

3. International Video Fittings

For global clients outside India, we establish private video consultation calls to verify measurements. We ship mock fits if required. Once the final customized cut is completed, minor alterations needed can be coordinated through local tailors, with refund vouchers provided up to a pre-agreed threshold.
