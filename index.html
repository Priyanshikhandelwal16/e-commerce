How We Work

From loom to your wardrobe

01

Loom & Weave

Pure Katan silk threads are woven on hand-looms in Varanasi — creating a base fabric that carries its own natural luster.

02

Kartoon Design

Ancestral design blueprints — called kartoons — are traced onto the stretched silk fabric by our senior design curations.

03

Zari Embroidery

Artisans spend 400–1100 hours threading real 22-karat gold zari wire through the silk — stitch by painstaking stitch.

04

Fitting & Care

Complimentary personalized fittings at our showrooms. Delivery in a custom preservation chest with thread warranty.

Our Story

Four generations of craftsmanship

Founded in the historic Johari Bazaar of Jaipur in 1921, Rang Mahal began as a modest trading post for handloom textiles. Over the decades, we brought in master karigars from Varanasi and Lucknow to establish in-house design ateliers.

Today, guided by the fourth generation of our family, we continue to preserve the hand-stitch techniques of Zardozi, Chikankari, and Gota Patti. Each piece we commission carries the soul of the artisan who made it.
